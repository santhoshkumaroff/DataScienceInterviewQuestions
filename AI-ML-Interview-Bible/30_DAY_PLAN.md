# ⚡ 30-Day Crash Plan: "This Is My Last Chance"

> "If it is important to you, you will find a way. If not, you will find an excuse."

This is the **high-intensity plan**. It assumes you can dedicate **8-10 hours daily** for 30 days. No excuses. No breaks.

---

## How This Plan Works

- Each day has **Morning (3h)**, **Afternoon (3h)**, **Evening (2h)** sessions.
- Total: ~8 hours/day active learning.
- Sundays: 5-hour revision + rest.
- Follow the order strictly.
