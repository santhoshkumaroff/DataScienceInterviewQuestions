# 🐢 60-Day Steady Preparation Plan

> "Slow and steady wins the race." — Aesop

This is a **relaxed 60-day plan** for people who have other commitments (college, job, family). You need **3-4 hours daily** — no more than 5 hours on any day. Weekends are lighter for revision.

---

## How This Plan Works

- **Daily effort:** 3-4 hours (flexible timing)
- **Weekly review:** Every Sunday is revision + rest
- **Daily structure:** Theory (1.5h) → Practice (1.5h) → Revision (1h)

## Sundays (Weekly Revision) — Template

For every Sunday:

```
Morning (2h):
  - Rewrite cheat sheet from memory for the week's topics
  - Identify gaps → re-study weak areas

Afternoon (1h):
  - Solve 5-10 practice problems (LeetCode or SQL)
  - Review one STAR story

Evening (1h):
  - Plan next week
  - Rest, recharge, do something fun
```
