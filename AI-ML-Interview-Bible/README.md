# 🧠 AI/ML Interview Bible

> Your complete guide to cracking AI/ML interviews as a fresher.

---

## What Is This Book?

The AI/ML Interview Bible is a **free, beginner-friendly** guide for anyone who wants to land their first job in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Whether you are a final-year student, a recent graduate, or someone switching careers into AI/ML — this book is for you.

## Target Audience

- **Freshers** looking for entry-level AI/ML jobs
- **Career switchers** moving into AI/ML from other fields
- **Students** preparing for campus placements or internships
- **Self-taught learners** who want structured interview preparation

## What's Inside

| Section | What You Will Learn |
|---------|---------------------|
| **ROADMAP.md** | A complete step-by-step learning path from zero to interview-ready |
| **INTERVIEW_STRATEGY.md** | How to approach interviews — before, during, and after |
| **30_DAY_PLAN.md** | A high-intensity "last chance" crash plan (30 days) |
| **60_DAY_PLAN.md** | A relaxed, steady preparation plan (60 days) |
| **HOW_TO_IMPRESS_INTERVIEWERS.md** | Soft skills, communication, body language, and confidence tips |
| **PROJECT_DISCUSSION_GUIDE.md** | How to talk about your projects like a pro |

## How To Use This Book

1. **Start with ROADMAP.md** — see where you stand and what you need to learn.
2. **Pick your timeline** — choose the 30-day or 60-day plan based on how much time you have.
3. **Read INTERVIEW_STRATEGY.md** before your first interview.
4. **Read HOW_TO_IMPRESS_INTERVIEWERS.md** and **PROJECT_DISCUSSION_GUIDE.md** a day before your interview.

> **Tip:** Bookmark this repo. Revisit it before every interview round.
